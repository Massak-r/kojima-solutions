Conference Theme

Key Research Areas

PASC 26 asks: how do we ensure trust in the methods, processes, and solutions derived through exascale-class co-design?

AI in Medicine & Healthcare

Confidence and reliability in computational diagnoses and medical simulations through rigorous co-design.

Extreme Weather Modelling

Model validity and reproducibility for climate simulation at exascale — trusted environmental forecasts.

Energy Assurance & Security

HPC co-design for reliable, resilient energy infrastructure modelling and grid-scale simulation.

Epidemiological Modelling

Trustworthy simulation for public health decision-making and disease modelling at population scale.

Digital Twins

Verifiable representations of complex physical systems — from cardiovascular engineering to aerospace.

Reproducibility & Open Science

Best practices for validated, reproducible results in HPC contexts using open data and software artifacts.
